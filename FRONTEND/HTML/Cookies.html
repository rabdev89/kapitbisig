What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help sites remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and understand how pages are being used — all to give you a better experience.

Your Rights

You can change your preferences at any time by clicking "Manage Preferences" in the cookie banner or footer. You also have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your data under the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act 10173).
