Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Welcome to Kapitbisig — a crowdfunding platform dedicated to empowering NGOs in Barangay 105, Tondo, Manila. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, protect, and share your personal information when you use our website and services. By accessing or using Kapitbisig, you agree to the practices described in this policy. We encourage you to read it carefully. If you have any questions, please contact our support team.

1
Overview

Introduction

Kapitbisig ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website located at www.kapitbisig.ph (the "Platform"). We are committed to safeguarding the personal information of our users — donors, NGO partners, volunteers, and community members — in accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) of the Philippines and internationally recognized data protection standards.

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the Kapitbisig Platform and covers all data collected through our website, donation forms, account registration, and any communication you have with us. It does not apply to third-party websites or services linked from our Platform.

By continuing to use Kapitbisig, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue your use of the Platform.

2
Data Collection

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, as well as information generated automatically when you use our Platform. The types of information we may collect include:

a) Information You Provide

  • Account Registration: Full name, email address, password, and optional profile photo when you create an account.
  • Donation Information: Billing name, email address, contact number, and payment details (processed securely through our payment gateway partners).
  • Communication Data: Messages, feedback, or inquiries you send to our support team through email, forms, or live chat.
  • NGO Partner Data: Organization details, registration documents, authorized representative information, and bank account details for fund disbursement.

b) Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, clicks, referral sources, and other interaction data.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, IP address, and device identifiers.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: As described in Section 7 of this Policy.

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as government IDs, health information, or biometric data unless explicitly required for NGO verification and with your express consent.

3
Data Usage

How We Use Information

We use the information collected to operate, maintain, and improve the Kapitbisig Platform. Specifically, your information is used for:

  • Processing Donations:To facilitate secure transactions and relay funds to the appropriate NGO partner.
  • Account Management: To create, authenticate, and manage your user account and donation history.
  • Sending Receipts and Notifications: To issue donation confirmations, transaction receipts, and campaign updates via email.
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries, resolve disputes, and provide assistance.
  • Legal Compliance: To meet our obligations under Philippine law, including the Data Privacy Act of 2012 and Anti-Money Laundering Act.
  • Platform Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve the design, functionality, and user experience of the Platform.
  • Communications: To send newsletters, impact reports, or platform updates — only with your consent, and with a clear option to unsubscribe at any time.

We will never use your personal data for purposes incompatible with those stated above, and we will not engage in profiling or automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects on you without your explicit consent.

4
Data Sharing

Sharing of Information

Kapitbisig does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • NGO Partners: When you make a donation, your name and donation amount may be shared with the recipient NGO for record-keeping and acknowledgment purposes, unless you have chosen to donate anonymously.
  • Payment Processors: Your payment details are shared with trusted, PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway partners solely to process your transaction. We do not store full card numbers.
  • Service Providers: We engage third-party vendors (e.g., cloud hosting, email delivery, analytics) who process data on our behalf and are contractually bound to protect it.
  • Legal Obligations: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government authority, or when necessary to protect the rights, safety, and property of Kapitbisig, our users, or the public.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or transfer of assets, your data may be transferred to the new entity, subject to equivalent privacy protections.

All third-party service providers are carefully vetted and required to maintain the confidentiality and security of your personal data in accordance with our data sharing agreements.

5
Security

Data Protection

We take the security of your personal information seriously and have implemented a range of technical and organizational measures to protect it from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

  • SSL/TLS Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols.
  • Secure Payment Processing: Payment data is handled exclusively by PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways. Card details are never stored on Kapitbisig servers.
  • Access Controls: Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only, on a strict need-to-know basis.
  • Regular Security Audits: We conduct periodic security reviews and vulnerability assessments to identify and address potential risks.
  • Data Minimization: We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our services and do not retain data longer than required.

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account and to notify us immediately if you suspect any unauthorized activity.

6
Your Rights

User Rights

Under the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173) and other applicable laws, you have the following rights regarding your personal information held by Kapitbisig:

Right to Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you and how it is being processed.

Right to Rectification

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data we hold about you.

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.

Right to Portability

Receive a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

Right to Object

Object to the processing of your data for direct marketing or other non-essential purposes.

Right to Damages

Seek compensation for any damages resulting from a breach of your data privacy rights.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@kapitbisig.ph. We will respond to all requests within 15 business days. You may also file a complaint with the National Privacy Commission (NPC) of the Philippines at privacy.gov.ph.

7
Tracking

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Kapitbisig uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on the Platform, analyze usage patterns, and deliver relevant content.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences and recognize you on subsequent visits.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the Platform to function properly, including maintaining your login session and enabling secure donations. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with the Platform (e.g., Google Analytics). Data collected is aggregated and anonymized.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and choices (e.g., language, anonymous donation preference) to personalize your experience.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used — only with your consent — to track visits and show you relevant content. You may opt out at any time.

Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies, delete existing cookies, or receive a notification when a new cookie is placed. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the Platform.

On your first visit, you will be presented with a cookie consent banner. Non-essential cookies will only be activated upon your acceptance. You may update your cookie preferences at any time via our cookie settings panel.
