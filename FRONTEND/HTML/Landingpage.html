UpSkills+ Foundation Inc.

Barangay 105, Tondo, Manila

Mission

UpSkills+ Foundation Inc. is committed to empowering individuals and communities in Barangay 105 through accessible education and practical skills development. The foundation believes that every resident — regardless of age, background, or circumstance — deserves the opportunity to learn, grow, and thrive. By bridging the gap between potential and opportunity, UpSkills+ helps individuals build the confidence and competencies needed to improve their lives and contribute meaningfully to their community.

Focus Areas

The foundation channels its efforts into programs that create measurable, lasting impact — prioritising those most in need within the barangay.

Literacy & Education Livelihood Training Youth Programs Scholarship Support Skills Development

BYSMP Bless the Children Foundation Inc.

Barangay 105, Tondo, Manila

Mission

BYSMP Bless the Children Foundation Inc. is dedicated to nurturing the holistic well-being of children and families in Barangay 105. Through compassionate programs in child welfare, health, and community development, the foundation strives to create a safe, supportive environment where every child can flourish. Rooted in the values of service, love, and solidarity, BYSMP works hand-in-hand with families and community leaders to break cycles of poverty and build a brighter future for the next generation.

Focus Areas

The foundation channels its resources toward child-centered initiatives that address the most pressing needs of families in the barangay.

Child Welfare Health & Nutrition Family Support Community Dev. Child Protection
